Designing, Defining, and Triggering CSS3
Transitions, Transforms, and Animations

This example demonstrates some standard HTML/JavaScript/Transition
events as well as some designing, defining, and triggering of CSS3
transitions, transforms, and animations.

We have some flip cards in a spinning div. The form demonstrates some
more events with a size-changing submit button and a triggered alert.
The entire page makes use of "onload" to fade in content.
